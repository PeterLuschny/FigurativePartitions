🧩 How to Play & Tips

🎮 How to play

  • Choose a target sum using the input box
  • Add shapes by clicking the buttons in the palette (each shape appears only once)
  • Adjust shapes: click + or – to change their size
  • Remove shapes: click ✕ to delete them
  • Every value-changing click counts toward your score!
  • 🎯 Reach the exact target to win — with click stats included

💡 Tips

  • Pebbles are fixed at value 1
  • Each shape has a unique value formula based on its type and size
  • Tooltips on buttons link to relevant OEIS entries
  • Shape buttons disable when used — plan wisely!
